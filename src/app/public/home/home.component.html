Welcome

Arbor Landing is a great community of 200 homes in the south part of Olathe, KS. With great access to shopping, parks, and in the Award Winning Olathe School District, Arbor Landing is a wonderful neighborhood to live in.



Parks and Trails

The City of Olathe's Arbor Landing Park is located in NorthWest corner of the subdivision. In addition, the neighborhood is close to the Indian Creek Trail system, offering miles of biking and walking paths.

Shopping

Grocery Store, Banks, and multiple restaurants all located within walking distance, making it easy to get the items you need

Schools

Located in the Olathe Public School District, providing residents access to some of the highest ranks public schools in country. Sunnyside Elementary School is located in the neighborhood, and Chisholm Trail Middle School just a short walk.
